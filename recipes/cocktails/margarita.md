# Two cocktails quick reference
* 2 doubles tequila
* 2 doubles tripe sec
* 1 doubles lime

# Ingredients single
* 50ml white tequila
* 40-50ml triple sec
* 25ml fresh squeezed lime juice
* 1 thin slice of lime

# Process
1. Salt rims of glasses
2. Put glasses in freezer
3. Shake ingredients with ice
4. Add ice cubes in glass
5. Pour cocktail(s)
